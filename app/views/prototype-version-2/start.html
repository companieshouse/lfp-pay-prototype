Pay your Companies House late filing penalty

You will need:

  • the company number
  • the invoice number
  • a credit or debit card to pay

The invoice number can be found in your Companies House remittance advice on your statement.

Other ways to pay your penalty

By phone

Telephone: 0303 1234 500
Paying your penalty through instalments can also be set up by phone.
