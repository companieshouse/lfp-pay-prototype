Pay your Companies House late filing penalty

You can use this service to pay a company's late filing penalty with a credit or debit card.

You'll need

  • your company number
  • the penalty reference shown on your penalty notification
  • a credit or debit card

Start now

Other ways to pay your penalty

By phone

You can pay by phone using your credit or debit card.
Telephone: 0303 1234 500
