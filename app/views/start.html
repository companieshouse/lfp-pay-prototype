You can use this service to pay a company's late filing penalty with a credit or debit card.

You cannot use this service to pay:

  • a penalty that has been taken to court
  • a penalty that has other legal fees
  • more than one penalty

You'll need:

  • the company number
  • the reference number on the penalty notice
  • an email address or Companies House account
  • a credit or debit card

It takes around 5 minutes to pay.
